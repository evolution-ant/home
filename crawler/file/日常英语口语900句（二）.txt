一、在日常对话聊天时，不免会对一些常见的事物进行提问，以下的日常英语口语就是关于谈论日常事物的：
61. Do you have a book? 你有一本书吗？
62. Yes,I do. 是的，我有。
63. You have a radio,don’t you? 你有一台收音机，对吗？
64. No,I don’t . 不，我没有。
65. I don’t have a phonograph,either. 我也没有留声机。
66. Dees this radio belong to you? 这台收音机是你的吗？
67. Yes,I think it does. 是的，我认为它是我的。
68. How many sisters and brothers do you have? 你有几个姊妹和兄弟？
69. Don’t you have my hat? 你没拿我的帽子吧？　
70. Yes,I have both your hat and your coat. 不，你的帽子和大衣都在我这里。　
71. Does John have a yellow pencil? 约翰有一支黄铅笔吗？　
72. Yes,he does. 是的，他有。　
73. He has a radio,doesn’t he? 他有一台收音机，对不对？　
74. No,he doesn’t have one. 不，他没有。　
75. He already has a phonograph,but he doesn’t have a radio yet. 他已经有一架留声机，但是他还没有收音机。　

二、下面这几句常用英语口语是关于约会的：
76. I'd like to make an appointment to see Mr. Cooper.我想约个时间去看库柏先生。
77. Would you like to arrange for a personal interview? 你愿意安排一次个人面试吗?
78. Your appointment will be next Thursday at 10 o'clock. 你的约见时间是下星期四10点。
79. I can come any day except Thursday.除了星期四，我什么时候都能来。
80. He wants to change his appointment from Monday to Wednesday.他想把他的约会从星期一改到星期三 
81. She failed to call the office to cancel her appointment. 她没有打电话给办公室取消她的约会。
82. I'm going to call the employment agency for a job.我要打电话给职业介绍所找份工作。 
83. Please fill in this application form.请填写这张申请表。 
84. Are you looking for a permanent position?你在找一个固定的职位吗? 
85. I'm going to call a plumber to come this afternoon.今天下午我要叫一个管道工来。 
86. I couldn't keep the appointment because I was sick.我不能赴约，因为我病了。 
87. I'm a new employee.I was hired yesterday. 我是新员工。我昨天才受雇的。
88. Please call before you come,otherwise we might not be home.你来之前请先打个电话，否则我们可能不在家。 
89. Let's make a date to go shopping next Thursday.让我们约定下星期四去购物。 
90. Will you please lock the door when you leave?你离开的时候，请把门锁上好吗?

三、如果你想约小伙伴做什么的话，可以用到下面这些日常英语口语：
91. What time are you going to get up tomorrow morning? 明天早上你打算什么时候起床
92. I'll probably wake up early and get up at 6:30.我可能醒得早，六点半起床。 
93. What will you do then? 那你会怎么做?
94. After I get dressed,I'll have breakfast.穿好衣服后，我要吃早餐 
95. What will you have for breakfast tomorrow morning?明天早饭你吃什么? 
96. I'll probably have eggs and toast for breakfast. 我早餐可能吃鸡蛋和烤面包。
97. After breakfast,I'll get ready ro go to work.吃完早饭，我就准备好去上班了。 
98. I'll leave the house at 8:00 and get to the office at 8:30.我8点离开家，8点30分到达办公室。 
99. I'll probably go out for lunch at about 12:30. 我大概在12:30左右出去吃午饭。
100. I'll finish working at 5:30 and get home by 6 o'clock.我将在5点半结束工作，6点钟到家。 
101. Are you going to have dinner at home tomorrow night? 明天晚上你打算在家吃晚饭吗?
102. Do you think you'll go to the movies tomorrow night? 你认为明天晚上你会去看电影吗

104. When I get sleepy,I'll probably get ready for bed.当我困的时候，我可能已经准备好睡觉了。 
105. Do you think you'll be able to go to sleep right away?你认为你马上就能睡着吗
