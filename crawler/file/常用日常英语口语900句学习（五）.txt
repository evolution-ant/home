一、关于购物的常用日常英语口语900句：
221.Ridiculous! I'd take it for 50 yuan.太荒谬了！（太贵了）如果50块我就买。
222.I'd buy this if it were cheaper。再便宜一点我就买。
223.Even if an article of clothing is very cute, but still seems overpriced for the fabric quality -- it is. 虽然一件衣服非常可爱，但是也会由于它的纤维质地而觉得价格过高。
224.I like everything about it except the price。这东西除了价钱之外我都喜欢。
225.If I buy more than one, will you give me a discount?多买一点有折扣吗?
226.I'm going to shopping, because I need clothes 我要去购物，因为我需要衣服。
227.If this shirt doesn't fit,may I bring it back later? 如果这件衬衫不合身，我可以把它带回来吗?
228.What size shoes do you wear? 你穿多大尺码的鞋子?
229.That suit looks perfect for you.那套西装看起来很适合你。 
230.This dress is made of silk,isn't it? 这件衣服是用丝绸制成的，是吗？
231.She asked the shopkeeper if she could try the sweater on. 她问店主她是否能试穿一下那件绒衣。 

232.Where people once dreamed of buying a new car, they now dream of buying a luxury model. 人们过去可能想要一辆新车，现在他们想要一辆豪华车。 
233.What's the price of that washing machine?洗衣机多少钱？ 
234.How much is this blanket? 这个毛毯多少钱？
235.Do you sell this towel today？今天出售这种毛巾吗？
236.This leather purse is beautiful, but it's too expensive这个皮革钱包很漂亮，但太贵了。 
237.Tell me what I owe you and I'll settle up.告诉我我欠你多少钱，我会付清的。
238.That will be twenty dollars and seventy cents.这是20美元70美分。 
239.Do you have change for a twenty-dollar bill?你有20美元零钱吗？
240.The clerk helped me find what I wanted.店员帮我找到了我想要的东西。 

二、关于兴趣爱好的常用日常英语口语900句学习：
241.I enjoy eating very much.我是非常喜欢吃。
242.What were you just doing?你刚才在做什么?
243.What are you doing just now?你刚才在做什么?
244.I was doing my exercise,it is called Tai Chi.我在做运动,它叫做太极拳。
245.You should try it,too.你也应该试试看。
246.I didn't know you did yoga.我不知道你会瑜珈。
247.You should try it.你应该试试。
248.We should get together more and have a nice chant.我们应该多聚一聚,好好聊—聊。
249.Swimming is not my cup of tea.游泳不是我的最爱。

250.A healthy hobby makes one relaxed and brings happiness健康的爱好使人轻松，帶来快乐。
251.I spend a lot of time on my hobby.我花了很多时间在我的兴趣爱好上。
252.Too much absorbed in one's hobby and neglecting work are not good.过度沉湎于自己的爱好而忽视工作是不好的。
253.I've always been interested in bungee jumping.我一向对蹦极很感兴趣。
254.Growing flowers is one of my favorite pastimes.养花是我最喜欢的消遣之一。
255.I love modern dance, and it always makes me feel full of vitality.我最喜欢跳舞，它总是让我感到充满活力。
256.What do you like doing in your spare time?你喜欢在业余时间做什么？
257.What do you do when you are not working?你不工作的时候做什么？
258.What do you like to do in your spare time?在业余时间你喜欢做什么？
259.How do you entertain yourself after work?下班之后你有什么娱乐活动？
260.That's really interesting. What other hobbies do you have that I don't know about?真是太有趣了。你还有其他什么我不知道的兴趣爱好吗？
